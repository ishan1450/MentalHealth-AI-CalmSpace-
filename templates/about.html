About CalmSpace

CalmSpace is an AI-powered mental health companion created to support emotional well-being. This assistant is trained to have compassionate, empathetic, and supportive conversations, designed especially for users in India.

Whether you're feeling anxious, stressed, lonely, or just need someone to talk to, CalmSpace is here to listen—anytime, anywhere.

Note: CalmSpace is not a replacement for professional help. If you or someone you know is in crisis, please seek help from licensed professionals or contact a mental health helpline.

Built with ❤️ using Python (Flask), HTML, CSS, JS, and a fine-tuned AI model (Llama 3b).
